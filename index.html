Metropolitan Police Department

Serve your community, protect Washington DC, and uphold the law. Join a professional and disciplined team today.

Why Join the MPD?

As a member of the MPD, you join a team that values discipline, integrity, and public service. You’ll receive world-class training and opportunities for career advancement.

Keep the city safe, guard critical locations, and respond to emergencies with precision. Your work directly impacts the safety of the nation’s capital.

Take part in tactical operations, investigations, VIP protection, and other specialized units that challenge your skills and develop leadership.

Be recognized for your dedication and excellence. Members of the MPD are respected professionals known as the “Keepers of Washington DC.”
